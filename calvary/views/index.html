Welcome to Our Church

We are a family of believers who seek to glorify God through worship, service, and love.

At Calvary Church, our mission is to be and make authentic followers of Jesus Christ — to worship God passionately, to preach and teach His Word faithfully, and to serve others in love as we share His Gospel throughout the world. We invite you to join us!
